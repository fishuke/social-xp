Social XP · Roleplay redesign v4

A date, not a dashboard.

Yoodli and Speeko frame roleplay as a recorded call with an analytics panel — corporate, cold, watched. Ours should feel like the actual moment: a dim bar, someone across the table, your phone in your hand. The convo IS the interface. Reactions replace metrics; the coach whispers, it never lectures.

Reactions, not analytics

One vibe bar

Coach whispers
one nudge at a time, dismissible
