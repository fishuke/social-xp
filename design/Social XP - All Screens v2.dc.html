Onboarding

Sell the belief in 20 seconds, capture one goal, set the pace. The streak mindset starts before the home screen.

02

Home — the Learn path

The journey path won. One scrollable road through Chapter 1 with named lessons, a reward chest, and a checkpoint boss. Daily quests sit on top — three small wins per session. Chapter banner taps through to the chapter list.

03

Chapters — the book, gamified

The content spine. Five chapters from first hello to leading a room, each ending in a real-world checkpoint. Opens from the chapter banner on Home.

04

The lesson loop — 5 beats, ~3 minutes

Concept → your call (tap to answer) → a quote you keep → the real-world rep → claim. Same rhythm every day, so the habit never needs willpower. Shown: Chapter 1, Lesson 3.

05

The quote collection

Every lesson ends in a quote you keep — pocket wisdom, one card at a time. Completing a chapter's set is the collector itch that brings people back. Cards are share-ready by design (free marketing).

06

The streak moment

Fires when the daily goal is done. A big number, the week laid out, and instant share.

07

You — XP & chapter progress

The mirror: total XP, streak, reps and quotes, plus the road through all five chapters. XP only goes up.

08

Premium · Social XP+

Free gets one lesson a day — enough to build the habit. Plus unlocks unlimited reps and the AI coaching that costs real money to run.

AI Coach — Version 2, unlocks with Chapter 2
